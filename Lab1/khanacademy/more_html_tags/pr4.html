Recipe №1

  • Time: 30 min to 3 hours
  • Serves: sour cream and dill
For filling:
Ingredients Quantity
Beets 2-3 medium-sized
Cabbage 1 small head
Potatoes 2-3 medium-sized
Carrots 2-3
Onions 1 large
Tomatoes 2 medium-sized
Garlic 2-3 cloves
Beef or vegetable broth 8 cups
Bay leaves 2
Salt and pepper to taste
Sugar 1-2 teaspoons

Step 1: Start by preparing the borscht dough. Peel and grate beets, then finely chop cabbage, potatoes, carrots, and onions. In a large pot, combine the vegetables with tomatoes, garlic, and bay leaves. Add beef or vegetable broth. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer until vegetables are tender.

Step 2: While the borscht is simmering, prepare a mixture of vinegar or lemon juice, adjusting to taste. Optionally, add sugar for balance. Serve the borscht hot, garnished with a dollop of sour cream if desired. Enjoy this hearty and flavorful soup!

Recipe №2

Fried Chicken

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: sauces
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken pieces (e.g., 1 kg)
Flour (e.g., 1 cup)
Salt (e.g., 1 tsp)
Pepper (e.g., 1/2 tsp)
Paprika (e.g., 1 tsp)
Buttermilk (e.g., 1 cup)
Vegetable oil (e.g., 2 cups)
Garlic powder (e.g., 1 tsp)
Onion powder (e.g., 1 tsp)
Dried herbs (optional) (e.g., 1 tsp)

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika. Coat chicken pieces with the seasoned flour mixture.

Step 2: Dip each flour-coated chicken piece into buttermilk, then dredge again in the flour mixture for a double coating.

Step 3: Heat vegetable oil in a pan. Fry chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes.

Step 4: In a separate bowl, mix garlic powder, onion powder, and dried herbs into the remaining buttermilk. Serve as a dipping sauce.

Step 5: Enjoy your crispy fried chicken with the flavorful dipping sauce!

Recipe №3

Fried Rice with Vegetables

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: sauces
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked rice (preferably day-old) 4 cups
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
Onion 1 pc, finely chopped
Carrots 1 cup, diced
Peas 1/2 cup, frozen
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Ginger 1 tsp, grated
Green onions 2 stalks, sliced
Sesame oil 1 tsp

Step 1: Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large pan. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent.

Step 2: Add diced carrots and cook for 3-4 minutes. Add frozen peas and continue cooking until vegetables are tender.

Step 3: Push the vegetables to the side, add a bit more oil if needed, and stir in minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for 1-2 minutes.

Step 4: Add cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Pour soy sauce over the rice and mix well.

Step 5: Stir in sliced green onions and drizzle with sesame oil. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, ensuring the rice is heated through.

Step 6: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade fried rice with vegetables!
